About Us Page

The Sickle cell Diaries is what we at SCD like to call "A Survivor"s guide to managing Sickle cell anemia".
SCD is predominant in the african community and we are devoted to creating awereness about the disease.
We aim to let sickle cell warriors know that they can lead a normal and fulfilling life
and this blog in a little way gives them the necessary information to do that.
